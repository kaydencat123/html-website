This page is all about special items i will only be doing the most popular items

Assault vest

Holders Special Defense is 1.5x, but it can only select damaging moves.

Choice Band

Holder's Attack is 1.5x, but it can only select the first move it executes.

Choice Scarf

Holder's Speed is 1.5 but, it can only select the first move it executes.

Choice Specs

Holder's Special Attack is 1.5x but, it can only use the first move it selects.

Clear Amulet

Prevents other pokemon from lowering the holders stat increases.

Covert Cloak

Holder is not affected by secondary effect of another Pokemon's attack.

Eject Button

If holer survives a hit, it immediately switches out to a chosen ally. Single Use

Eject Pack

If the holder's stat stages are lowered, it switches to a chosen ally. Single Use

Focus Sash

If holder's HP is full, will survive an attack that would KO it with 1 hp. Single Use

Leftovers

At the end of every turn holder heals 1/16 of its max HP.

Life Orb

Holder's attacks do 1.3x damage, and it loses 1/10 its max HP after the attack.

Mental Herb

Cures holder of Attract, Disable, Encore, Healblock, Taunt or Torment. Single Use

Power Herb

Holder's two-turn moves complete in one turn. except Sky Drop. Single Use

Rocky Helmet

If holder is hit by a contact move, the attacker loses 1/6 of its max hp.

Safety Goggles

Holder is immune to powder moves and damage from Sandstorm Or Hail

Sitrus Berry

Restores 1/4 Max HP When at 1/2 max HP or less. Single Use

Weakness Policy

If holder is hit super effectively, raises Attack, Special Attack by 2 stages. Single Use
